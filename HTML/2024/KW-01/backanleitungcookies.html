What we bake

Cookies not specialy healthy but they are delicious. Lets make some delicous cookies, outside hard inside soft. Happy baking!

Ingredients

2 baking trays
Baking paper
110g soft Butter
175g Sugar
¼ parcel Vanillasugar
¼ tsp Salt
1 Egg
150g Chocolate
160g Flour
1 pinch Baking powder

Recipe

1. Stir butter until peaks form. Add sugar, vanilla sugar, salt and egg, stir until the mixture is light. Stir in the chocolate. Mix the flour and baking powder, sift in, stir into a smooth dough.

2. Using 2 teaspoons, place balls of dough the size of a walnut on the prepared trays. Using cold-rinsed hands, flatten slightly so that they are 1 cm high and refrigerate for 15 minutes.

3. Preheat the oven to 155°C hot air (175°C top/bottom heat). Make sure you sweep the surfaces in half the time. ATTENTION: If you use top/bottom heat, add the sheets one after the other.

4. Bake in the center of the preheated oven for 11-13 minutes.

1 piece contains: Energy: 301kJ / 72kcal, fat: 4g, carbohydrates: 9g, protein: 1g

Tips & Tricks

1.To make the cookies nice and soft on the inside, it's best to melt the butter. ...

2.The dough should not be too firm and should not crumble. ...

3. To get evenly sized cookies, use an ice cream scoop to place dough balls on the baking sheet
